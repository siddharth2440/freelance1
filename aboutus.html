About Us

Consumer Saarthi is an online communication platform which is trying to focus the unfocused situations and problems. We emerged after witnessing the troubles faced by a common person in dealing with various brands, organisations & government authorities. The idea is to help the people by providing them with the best possible solution by the medium of learned lawyers & consultants. Being a communication platform we are broadly connected with lots of learned people coming from various streams of life like lawyers from High Court to Supreme Court, Media, Social Activist, Educationist, Ex-Bureaucrats, Chartered Accountants. We closely work with lots of MNC & Indian Brands to constitute a resolution platform for the common person.

At Consumer Saarthi, we are committed to providing our consumers with the highest quality assistance and guidance. Our team of experts works tirelessly to ensure that your needs are met and that you receive the best possible service.

At Consumer Saarthi, our team of expert lawyers specializes in handling cybercrime consumer complaints, consumer court matters, and other relevant issues. We provide personalized services to our clients through phone and email interactions, ensuring that you receive the guidance you need to resolve your complaints.

Our fees are calculated based on a 3% processing fee and an 18% GST charge.
