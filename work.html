What is Consumer Sarthi?
Consumer Sarthi is a registered NGO that was established to help people deal with various brands, organizations, and government authorities. Our mission is to provide a resolution platform for common people who face troubles while dealing with these entities. We work closely with many MNCs and Indian brands to ensure that our clients receive the best possible service and support.
Why Consumer Sarthi?
Consumer Sarthi is a team of professionals who work online to resolve your matter with the brand. In most cases, you don’t have to go anywhere unnecessary and waste your time, money, and energy. Our team will work by escalating your matter online from drafting your case to escalating it to the appropriate authorities or brands online and resolving it online.
What is your refund policy?
Consumer Sarthi provides online support to help people overcome their problems. Our team of learned lawyers is available to assist you over calls and email. We offer our services for 21 days and charge a service fee to maintain the quality of service we provide.
Does Consumer Sarthi really help?
Consumer Sarthi have proven to be the most effective way to provide a remedy to Consumers and address their grievances.
What type of services does Consumer Sarthi provide?
Consumer Sarthi is a platform that communicates with brands, organizations, or opposing parties on your behalf to resolve consumer issues amicably.
How can we track our complaint status?
To track the status of your complaint, you can call the number provided in your email to speak with your legal associate or consultant.
How can I make a Consumer Sarthi fees payment?
You can pay the fees online via the Payment link we share with you on your phone or email using a debit card, credit card, or net banking options & UIP.
How much you will take to resolve complaint?
The resolution of your complaint depends on the company’s response time. We cannot estimate how long it will take to resolve your complaint. However, we assure you that we will work diligently on your case for up to 21 days, depending on the matter/case.
